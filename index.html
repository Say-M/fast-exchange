Background Story

Fast Exchange began as an experimental cryptocurrency with a single goal of getting on as many exchanges as fast as it can! It will focus on the single most important growth phase of a cryptocurrency - the marketing.

We are all about creating a hype, building both organic and community/developer driven marketing efforts. We not only will be on all exchanges at an astonishing pace, but also being listed on all the aggregate sites,

such as CoinMarketCap, CoinGecko, CoinHunt and so on. We will target all the well-known influencers, call channels in the space to raise awareness.

By starting out as a hype/meme coin, we generate a large following to eventually transition our utility use case - to become the fastest, most comprehensive exchange!
